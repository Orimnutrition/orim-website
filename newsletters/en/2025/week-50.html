50

December 15-21, 2025

EDITORIAL

The winter solstice approaches, marking the longest night and the return of light. This week, we honor the ancient connection between the solstice and renewal by exploring the science of autophagy, the cellular recycling process that removes damaged components and rejuvenates every cell in the body.

01

SMARTFARMING & AGRITECH

Fasting for 24-36 Hours Activates Deep Autophagy

A study in Nature Cell Biology (Dec 2025) used advanced imaging to show that autophagy activation begins at 12 hours of fasting, reaches moderate levels at 24 hours, and achieves deep cellular recycling (including mitophagy) at 36 hours. The process clears senescent proteins, damaged mitochondria, and intracellular debris.

Spermidine-Rich Foods Mimic Fasting Autophagy

A clinical trial at the University of Graz (Dec 2025) demonstrated that dietary spermidine (from wheat germ, aged cheese, mushrooms, and soybeans) activates autophagy pathways without fasting. Participants consuming 1.2mg spermidine daily showed 20% improvement in cardiac function and 15% reduction in inflammatory biomarkers.

Autophagy Declines 50% After Age 60

Research in Aging Cell (Dec 2025) quantified the age-related decline in autophagy: compared to age 30, autophagic flux at age 60 is 50% lower and at age 80 is 75% lower. This decline correlates with accumulation of damaged proteins (Alzheimer's amyloid, Parkinson's alpha-synuclein) and mitochondrial dysfunction.

02

BIOLOGICAL ADVANCES

Autophagy: The Body's Winter Cleaning

Yoshinori Ohsumi's Nobel Prize-winning discovery of autophagy mechanisms (2016) has spawned a decade of clinical research. A 2025 comprehensive review in Cell demonstrated that autophagy performs 4 critical functions: (1) removes damaged mitochondria (mitophagy), (2) degrades misfolded proteins, (3) recycles cellular components for energy during fasting, and (4) destroys intracellular pathogens. Enhancing autophagy through fasting, exercise, and specific nutrients is now considered a cornerstone of longevity science.

The solstice fast: consider a 24-hour fast from dinner on the solstice to dinner the next day. This activates deep autophagy and symbolically aligns with the ancient tradition of renewal at the darkest moment.

THIS WEEK IN BRIEF

► Spermidine: Wheat germ contains 24mg spermidine per 100g, the richest food source. Two tablespoons daily provides a meaningful autophagic stimulus (Autophagy, 2025).

► Exercise: High-intensity exercise activates autophagy in skeletal muscle within 30 minutes, clearing damaged mitochondria and improving cellular energy production (EMBO J, 2025).

► Coffee: Coffee polyphenols (chlorogenic acid) activate autophagy independent of caffeine. Both regular and decaf coffee trigger cellular recycling within 4 hours (Cell Rep, 2025).
